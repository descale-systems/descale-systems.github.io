Descale is a small London-based quarterly meeting of people involved in web operations and development. We solve scaling problems by open discussion and continuous review.

Meetings take the format of multiple round-table discussions about topics nominated & voted on by the attendees in advance, with break-out tables for spontaneous debate. Everything said by attendees is under Chatham House Rule, and we encourage people to take and publish notes.

We aim to ensure that people knowledgeable about the chosen subjects are on-hand to offer advice and answer questions. If you think you know a lot about an upcoming topic, please let us know.

If you'd like to attend Descale, please sign up to our mailing list, follow us on twitter, or join our IRC channel.
